# Thrushcombe St Mary — portrait prompts

Image-generation prompts for every soul's sepia portrait. **Prepend the shared STYLE block to
each person's line.** Save each result as the person's index (`18.jpg`) **or** their name-slug
(`major-pringle.jpg`) in `portraits/` — the web server's `resolve_portrait` accepts either, any
image format. They appear on `/folk/<n>`, as little faces on the map, and as Discord avatars.

There's also a separate **map** prompt at the bottom (save as `portraits/map.jpg`).

---

## STYLE — prepend to every prompt

> *A 1934 sepia studio portrait photograph, rural West-Country England. Authentic period
> photography — soft silver-gelatin sepia tones, fine grain, slight vignette, plain muted studio
> backdrop. Head-and-shoulders, the sitter looking just off-camera. A real, unglamorous period
> face; period-accurate clothing, hair and grooming. Honest and dignified, of its time. No modern
> elements, no colour, no text, no border.*
